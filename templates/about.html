About UGV Blood Bank

UGV Blood Bank is dedicated to saving lives by providing safe and timely blood donations. We are committed to serving the community by ensuring a reliable supply of blood for patients in need. Our team works tirelessly to manage blood donations, organize drives, and make sure that no one is ever without the life-saving resource they need. Our goal is to provide help and hope to those in need, 24/7.

Our mission is to ensure that blood is always available for those who need it, especially in critical situations. Together, we make a difference by providing a vital resource.
